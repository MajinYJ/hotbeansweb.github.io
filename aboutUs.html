Hot Beans Web Development: Building the Future of the Web


In 2010, a young man called Phillip Heinz who had a passion for web development decided to set up a business and name it ‘Hot Beans Web’ which was because of his surname being Heinz. It is currently operating as a small office in Chelsea and the growth of the company has been slow for the past 13 years, so we are looking to take it to the next step by hiring several talented employees with the intention of becoming a large co-operation, working with several large companies. The main aim of Hot Beans Web is to be able to provide the best possible service to our clients which means that we will not stop until they match your request, which is usually creating high quality sites for people and businesses with their ideal vision in mind.

Goals

Our goal at Hot Beans is to build the future of the World Wide Web as we know it, by utilising new web technologies to build the websites of the world's biggest brands.

Partners

At Hot Beans, we pride ourselves in the brands and companies we've worked with and collaborated with to achieve our goals. We've worked with Microsoft, Mimo, GitHub and Adobe to help on web based projects and we plan to expand this within the near future.
